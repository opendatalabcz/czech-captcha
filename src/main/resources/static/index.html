CzechCaptcha

open-source free CAPTCHA system

Secures your website

CzechCaptcha can secure your web pages from frauds and abuses done by malicious bots for free. It does so by presenting a task that is easy to solve for human users but hard for computers. By securing your website you also help other people annotate their data which can then be used for example to train AI.

Choose from 4 different configurable CAPTCHA tasks:

  • solve equation
  • rewrite text
  • classify images
  • detect objects in image
  • implement your own task

Annotates your data

CzechCaptcha allows you to have your data annotated by humans for free. It is done by using it in the CAPTCHA tasks. This way the users' precious effort to solve the tasks is transformed into a value in the form of adding labels to images, videos, or sounds. By annotating your data you also help other people protect their websites from malicious bots.

Annotate your data with custom labels:

  • classify images
  • detect objects in images
  • implement your own data annotation mechanism
